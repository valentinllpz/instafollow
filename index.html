How to get your files

  1. 1

    Open Instagram → Settings → Your activity → Download your information

  2. 2

    Choose date range All time, pick any format (HTML or JSON), then request the download

  3. 3

    Once Instagram emails you, download and unzip the file. Open the folder connections/followers_and_following/

  4. 4

    Upload followers_1.html (or .json) and following.html (or .json) below — everything runs locally in your browser, nothing leaves your device
